Smaller Projects

There have been multiple small projects I have made over the course of my learning at NCTC. I have made smaller versions of games like puchinko and soccer. I have also made a two player tank game with the goal of defeating the other player. All of those projects I made in Unity, but I also made some in Unreal too. I have mad e a game where you run around and collect objects with various effects, and a game in which the player bounces a ball of a paddle to destroy as many bricks at the top of the screen as possible. I made these projects to not only familiarize myself with the inner workings of the engines, but also what they can or can't do.
